  • decisionStart skos:scopeNote "This property includes document subdivisions other than pages and is, therefore, broader than schema:pageStart. This property is necessary because most cases that appear in reporters begin on a page other than the first page. One case reporter volume contains dozens of cases. The beginning of the "work" (i.e., the case reporter) would be page one, but the beginning of every decision, except the first decision that appears in the case reporter, would necessarily be a page other than the first page." @en
